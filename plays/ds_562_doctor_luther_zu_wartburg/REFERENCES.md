# Doctor Luther at the Wartburg: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Doctor Luther zu Wartburg_, legend no. 562 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Martin Luther translates the Bible in his chamber and throws his inkwell to drive away the tempting devil.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 562.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
