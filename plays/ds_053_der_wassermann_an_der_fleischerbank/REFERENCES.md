# The Water Sprite at the Butcher's Bench: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Wassermann an der Fleischerbank_, legend no. 53 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A butcher cuts the finger of a water sprite who always pays for meat with holed coins, causing him to vanish.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 53.

| Source             | Key Work / Event        | Scope                                 |

| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 53 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
