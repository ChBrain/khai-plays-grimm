# Reinhart's Defense: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Reinhart's Defense_, an episode from Jacob Grimm's _Reinhart Fuchs_. Reinhart travels to the court and confesses his sins to Grimbart the Badger.

## Origin

Jacob Grimm's edition of the animal epic _Reinhart Fuchs_ (1834).

| Source          | Key Work / Event        | Scope                                           |
