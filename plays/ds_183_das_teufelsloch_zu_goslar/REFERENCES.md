# The Devil's Hole in Goslar: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Das Teufelsloch zu Goslar_, legend no. 183 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A violent dispute in Goslar is incensed by the devil, leaving a hole in the church wall that requires a cat to seal.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 183.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
