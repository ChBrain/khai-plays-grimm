# Adalbert of Babenberg: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Adalbert von Babenberg_, legend no. 468 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Archbishop Hatto tricks Count Adalbert with a literal breakfast ruse to execute him despite a safe conduct vow.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 468.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
