# God's Food: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Gottes Speise_, legend no. 362 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A boy lost in a snowstorm is protected from the cold and fed by a mysterious messenger sent from heaven.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 362.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |
| ------------------ | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Beyond the legend**: the production stages only what the legend records, not the wider history or tradition it touches.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
