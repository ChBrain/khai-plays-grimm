# The Rammelsberg: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Rammelsberg_, legend no. 475 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Emperor Otto's hunter Ramm discovers the Harz silver mines when his tied horse uncovers ore with its hooves.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 475.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
