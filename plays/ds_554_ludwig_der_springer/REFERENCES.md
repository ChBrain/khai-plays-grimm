# Ludwig the Springer: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Ludwig der Springer_, legend no. 554 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Ludwig escapes execution by leaping from the high tower of Gibichenstein Castle into the Saale river.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 554.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
