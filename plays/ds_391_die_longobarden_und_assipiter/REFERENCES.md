# The Lombards and the Assipiters: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die Longobarden und Aßipiter_, legend no. 391 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. The Goths use a clever dog-headed warrior scare to bypass Assipiter borders, resolved in single combat.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 391.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
