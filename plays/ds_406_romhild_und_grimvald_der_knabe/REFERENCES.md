# Romhild and Grimoald the Boy: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Romhild und Grimoald der Knabe_, legend no. 406 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Romhild betrays Friuli for love of the Hun king Cacan, who later cruelly executes her for treachery.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 406.
