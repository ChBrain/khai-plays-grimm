# Kaiser Karl at Nuremberg: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Kaiser Karl zu Nürnberg_, legend no. 22 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Kaiser Karl sits in the deep well of Nuremberg Castle, his beard grown through a stone table.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 22.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
