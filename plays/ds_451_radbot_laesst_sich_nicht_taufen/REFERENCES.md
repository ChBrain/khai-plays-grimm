# Radbod Refuses Baptism: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Radbot lässt sich nicht taufen_, legend no. 451 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Duke Radbod pulls his foot out of the baptismal font to avoid spending eternity apart from his pagan ancestors.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 451.
