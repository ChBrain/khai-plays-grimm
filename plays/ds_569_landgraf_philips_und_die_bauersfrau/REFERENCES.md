# Landgrave Philip and the Peasant Woman: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Landgraf Philips und die Bauersfrau_, legend no. 569 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Philip travels incognito, pays a poor woman's tax, and hears her wish that he had the hot gold in his belly.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 569.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |
| ------------------ | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 569 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
