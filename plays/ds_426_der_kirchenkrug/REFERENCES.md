# The Vase of Soissons: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Kirchenkrug_, legend no. 426 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Clovis avenges a broken church vase by slaying the proud warrior who struck it with an axe.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 426.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Beyond the legend**: the production stages only what the legend records, not the wider history or tradition it touches.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
