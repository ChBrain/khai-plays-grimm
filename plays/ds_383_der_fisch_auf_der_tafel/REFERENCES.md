# The Fish on the Table: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Fisch auf der Tafel_, legend no. 383 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Guilt-ridden King Theodoric mistakes a served fish head for his executed servant Symmachus and falls mortally ill.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 383.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
