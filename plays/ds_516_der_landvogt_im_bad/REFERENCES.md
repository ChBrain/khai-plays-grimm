# The Bailiff in the Bath: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Landvogt im Bad_, legend no. 516 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Wolfenschiessen tries to force Conrad's wife into his bath; Conrad returns and kills him with an axe.
