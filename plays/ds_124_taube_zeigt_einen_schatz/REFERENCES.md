# Dove Reveals a Treasure: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Taube zeigt einen Schatz_, legend no. 124 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Duke Heinrich of Breslau follows a dove in Krakow cathedral and finds a hidden treasure under the floorboards.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 124.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
