# Quedl the Little Dog: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Quedl das Hündlein_, legend no. 489 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Princess Mathild avoids the Devil's claim by staying awake with the help of her loyal barking dog Quedl.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 489.
