# Wittekind's Baptism: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Wittekinds Taufe_, legend no. 453 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Duke Wittekind spies on Charlemagne in beggar's garb, sees a miracle during Mass, and accepts baptism.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 453.
