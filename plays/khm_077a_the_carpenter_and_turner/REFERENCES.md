# The Carpenter and Turner: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Vom Schreiner und Drechsler_ (KHM 77a), an early tale withdrawn from the Grimms' _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_ after the first edition (1812). A prince abducts a princess with artificial wings, escapes the stake, and claims his kingdom.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_, first edition (1812), tale KHM 77a. An early tale withdrawn from the later editions. It opens: "Ein Schreiner und ein Drechsler sollten ihr Meisterstück machen..."
