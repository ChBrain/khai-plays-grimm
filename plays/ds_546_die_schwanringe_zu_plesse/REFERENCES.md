# The Swan Rings of Plesse: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die Schwanringe zu Plesse_, legend no. 546 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Three brothers build Plesse Castle, guided by their hunter bastard brother Heiso.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 546.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
