# The Saxons and the Thuringians: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die Sachsen und die Thüringer_, legend no. 416 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A Saxon youth tricks a Thuringian by exchanging gold chains for land, scattering gold dust to claim a wide territory.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 416.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |
| ------------------ | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 416 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Beyond the legend**: the production stages only what the legend records, not the wider history or tradition it touches.
