# The White Dove: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die weiße Taube_ (KHM 64a), an early tale withdrawn from the Grimms' _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_ after the first edition (1812). An underrated prince follows a fruit-stealing dove and frees a little man and a princess.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_, first edition (1812), tale KHM 64a. An early tale withdrawn from the later editions. It opens: "Vor eines Königs Pallast stand ein prächtiger Birnbaum, der trug jedes Jahr die schönsten Früchte..."
