# The Cursed Land Surveyors: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die verwünschten Landmesser_, legend no. 285 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Deceitful land surveyors are cursed to wander after death as will-o-the-wisps guarding the borders they manipulated.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 285.
