# The Bell Tolls by Itself: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Glocke läutet von selbst_, legend no. 266 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Self-tolling church and house bells warn citizens of a town of their impending death or that of their loved ones.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 266.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |
| ------------------ | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 266 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Beyond the legend**: the production stages only what the legend records, not the wider history or tradition it touches.
