# The Spinner at the Cross: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die Spinnerin am Creuz_, legend no. 179 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A poor woman spins yarn diligently for years to earn enough money to build a stone shrine near Vienna.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 179.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
