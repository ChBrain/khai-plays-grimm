# The Harschhoerner of Lucerne: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die Lucerner Harschhörner_, legend no. 520 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Charlemagne grants Lucerne the right to bear brass war horns in battle.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 520.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
