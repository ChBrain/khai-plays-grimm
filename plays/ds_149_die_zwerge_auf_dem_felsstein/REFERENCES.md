# The Dwarves on the Boulder: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die Zwerge auf dem Felsstein_, legend no. 149 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Dwarves who watch haymakers from a boulder are cruelly burned when pranksters heat the stone red-hot.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 149.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |
| ------------------ | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 149 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
