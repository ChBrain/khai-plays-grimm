# The Stag of Magdeburg: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Hirsch zu Magdeburg_, legend no. 445 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Charlemagne releases a stag with a golden collar, which is only recaptured centuries later by Barbarossa.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 445.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
