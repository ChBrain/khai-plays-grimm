# The Lady of Hahn and the Nix: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die Frau von Hahn und der Nix_, legend no. 69 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A noblewoman helping a nix-wife under the river refuses ashes, earning three gold pieces that sustain her house.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 69.

| Source             | Key Work / Event        | Scope                                 |
| ------------------ | ----------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 69 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
