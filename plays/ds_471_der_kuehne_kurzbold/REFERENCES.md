# The Bold Kurzbold: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der kühne Kurzbold_, legend no. 471 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Kuno 'Kurzbold', a small but brave hero, defeats rebel dukes during a chess match and slays an escaped court lion.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 471.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 471 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
