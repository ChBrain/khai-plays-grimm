# Charlemagne's Return from Hungary: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Carls Heimkehr aus Ungerland_, legend no. 444 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Charlemagne's signet ring dropped in a cup rescues Queen Hildegard from a forced remarriage.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 444.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
