# King Louis's Rib Rattles: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _König Ludwigs Rippe klappt_, legend no. 464 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. King Louis hides a severe rib injury during a campaign, his rib audibly rattling as he rides.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 464.
