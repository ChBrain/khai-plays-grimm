# Pigeon Announces the Enemy: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Taube sagt den Feind an_, legend no. 484 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A pigeon flies into a besieged city with a warning message tied to its neck, saving it from a surprise attack.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 484.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
