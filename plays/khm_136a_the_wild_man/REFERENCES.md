# The Wild Man: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _De wilde Mann_ (KHM 136a), an early tale withdrawn from the Grimms' _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_ after the first edition (1812). An enchanted wild man helps a banished prince win battles and inherit a kingdom.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_, first edition (1812), tale KHM 136a. An early tale withdrawn from the later editions. It opens: "Et was emoel en wilden Mann, de was verwünsket un genk bie de Bueren in den Goren (Garten) un in’t Korn un moek alles do Schande."
