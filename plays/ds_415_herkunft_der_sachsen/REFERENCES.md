## Line of Work

Staging _Herkunft der Sachsen_, legend no. 415 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Alexander's surviving veterans settle in Saxony, sparing local peasants and binding them as serfs.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
