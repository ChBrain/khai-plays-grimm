# The Salt Journeyman in Pomerania: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Salzknecht in Pommern_, legend no. 121 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A witch uses magic to pursue a fleeing salt journeyman, sending a black goat to slip between his legs.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 121.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
