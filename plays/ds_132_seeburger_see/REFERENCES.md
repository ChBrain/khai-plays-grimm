# Seeburger Lake: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Seeburger See_, legend no. 132 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. The wicked lifestyle of Count Isang causes his castle to sink, creating the Seeburger lake near Goettingen.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 132.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
