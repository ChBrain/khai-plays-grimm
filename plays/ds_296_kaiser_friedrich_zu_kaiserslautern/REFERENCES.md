# Emperor Frederick at Kaiserslautern: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Kaiser Friedrich zu Kaiserslautern_, legend no. 296 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A man let down on a rope into a deep cave encounters Emperor Frederick sitting on a golden throne with his knights.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 296.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
