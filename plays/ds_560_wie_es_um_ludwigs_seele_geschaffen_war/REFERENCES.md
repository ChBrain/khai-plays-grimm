# How Ludwig's Soul Fared: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Wie es um Ludwigs Seele geschaffen war_, legend no. 560 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A priest conjures the devil to show Ludwig the Mild that his father's soul is tormented on a red-hot iron throne.

## Origin
