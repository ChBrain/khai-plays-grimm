# The Wild Hunter and the Tailor: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der wilde Jäger und der Schneider_, legend no. 173 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A tailor in Münster mocks the wild hunter and gets knocked unconscious by a ghostly horse foot flying in.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 173.
