# Agilulf and Theudelind: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Agilulf und Theudelind_, legend no. 404 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Queen Theodelind chooses Agilulf as king, proposing with a kiss during their meeting at Laumell.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 404.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
