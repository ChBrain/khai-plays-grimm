# Emperor Henry the Fowler: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Kaiser Heinrich der Vogeler_, legend no. 470 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Duke Henry of Saxony is found catching birds in the woods when the princes arrive to elect him Emperor.
