# The Misfortune: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Das Unglück_ (KHM 175a), an early tale withdrawn from the Grimms' _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_ after the first edition (1812). A poor man tries to flee misfortune but is chased by wolves and water and crushed by a wall.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_, first edition (1812), tale KHM 175a. An early tale withdrawn from the later editions. It opens: "Wen das Unglück aufsucht, der mag sich aus einer Ecke in die andere verkriechen und gehen, wohin er will, es weiß ihn dennoch zu finden."
