# The Summer and Winter Garden: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Von dem Sommer- und Wintergarten_ (KHM 68a), an early tale withdrawn from the Grimms' _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_ after the first edition (1812). A merchant promises his youngest daughter to a beast for a rose from a summer-and-winter garden.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_, first edition (1812), tale KHM 68a. An early tale withdrawn from the later editions. It opens: "Ein Kaufmann wollte auf die Messe gehen, da fragte er seine drei Töchter, was er ihnen mitbringen sollte."
