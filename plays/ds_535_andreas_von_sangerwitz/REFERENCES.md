# Andreas of Sangerwitz: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Andreas von Sangerwitz, Comthur auf Christburg_, legend no. 535 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Andreas counsels peace before the Battle of Tannenberg but is mocked, later fighting to the death.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 535.
