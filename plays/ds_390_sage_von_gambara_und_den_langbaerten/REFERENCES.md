# Saga of Gambara and the Longbeards: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Sage von Gambara und den Langbärten_, legend no. 390 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Led by Gambara, the exiled Goths refuse to pay Vandal tribute and resolve to fight for their freedom.

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 390.
