# Amalaberga of Thuringia: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Amalaberga von Thüringen_, legend no. 550 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Amalaberga goads Hermenfried into war by half-covering the dining table.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 550.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
