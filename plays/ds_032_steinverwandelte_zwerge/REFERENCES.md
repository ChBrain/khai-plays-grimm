# Dwarfs Turned to Stone: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Steinverwandelte Zwerge_, legend no. 32 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. In a valley near Elnbogen, helpful dwarfs are turned to stone by their angry ruler when they go to a wedding.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 32.
