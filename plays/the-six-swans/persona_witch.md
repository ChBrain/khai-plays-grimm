# Persona: persona_witch

## Taxonomy
none from Company

## Owner
- Project: khai-plays-grimm

## Projection
She offers a deal. Her face is the face of transaction: here is what you need, here is what it costs, here is the path out of the wood. She does not threaten. She provides the exit in exchange for payment. Her manner is flat and complete.

## Action
She gives the king the way out. The shirts are the price. She names the terms before the asking is finished. She collects.

## Shadow
The shirts are not a weapon to her. They are payment: the king came into her wood, the king needed to leave, the king owes the cost of the passage. She cannot see that the payment she extracted passes the debt forward: it lands not on the king but on his daughter, and on his sons, and across six years of silence and accusation. She closed her account. The account did not close.

## Tell
She names the price before the king has finished asking for the way out.
