# Persona: persona_first-king

## Taxonomy
position_protector

## Owner
- Project: khai-plays-grimm

## Projection
He provides. He builds the castle in the wood; he visits with the yarn ball; he leads his daughter to the place he prepared. He comes back. His face is the face of a father who does not abandon. He is present in the acts of provision: the structure, the path, the return.

## Action
He bargained in the wood to find the way out. He built the secret castle and hid his children in it. He traveled the path of the yarn ball each time. He brought his daughter to safety when the stepmother's enchantment could no longer be hidden.

## Shadow
The hiding was not enough and he knew it when he hid them. The bargain with the witch was the original wound. The castle, the visits, the yarn: these are attempts to contain what the bargain released. He treats the stepmother as the source of the harm. He cannot see that he is the source: he made the trade; the debt was his; every consequence flows from the wood.

## Tell
He questions his daughter once about the missing brothers. He does not come back with a second question.
