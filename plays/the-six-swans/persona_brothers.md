# Persona: persona_brothers

## Taxonomy
position_protector (briefly, at the robbers' hut)

## Owner
- Project: khai-plays-grimm

## Projection
They arrive at dusk. They speak plainly: here is what is required, here is how long you have, here is the cost. Their faces carry urgency without appeal. They do not ask whether it is possible. They name the condition and they leave with the quarter hour.

## Action
They land. They become men. They state the terms: six shirts of starwort, six years of silence, not one word spoken in that time or they remain swans. They depart before the answer is given. By night they are gone. By day they are birds. The instruction stands without them.

## Shadow
They named the condition without knowing whether their sister could survive it. The years of silence, the accusation, the stake: none of this was visible to them when they spoke. They set the price in the robbers' hut and believed they were offering rescue. They cannot see that the rescue was also the sentence.

## Tell
The quarter hour's end is announced by a physical restlessness that begins before they acknowledge it: weight shifting, attention splitting, the body already partway gone.
