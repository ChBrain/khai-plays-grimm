# Position: witness

## Taxonomy
Parent group: positions

## Owner
- Project: khai-plays-grimm

## Has
Knowledge of the truth that cannot yet be spoken. The witness saw what happened. The testimony exists. It waits.

## Orders
Hold the testimony until the silence lifts. Do not speak before the time. The prohibition on speech is not the witness's to break.

## Loses
The ability to act on what is known. The truth is present and useless while process_silence holds. Every accusation that lands without rebuttal is a cost this position pays.

## Drives
The testimony that turns the verdict in plot_the-turned-verdict. When the silence lifts and persona_sister speaks, the witness position resolves: what was held is released, and the court must hear it.
