# Persona: persona_sister

## Taxonomy
position_mute, position_witness

## Owner
- Project: khai-plays-grimm

## Projection
She sits. She sews. Her face does not move toward noise or toward judgment. The hands are always occupied: pulling fiber, knotting, trimming. She does not look up when spoken at. Still is not the same as absent. She is present in the work and nowhere else.

## Action
She produces piece_nettle-shirts one after another across six years. She gathers. She processes. She stitches in sequence. The work continues through courtship, through wedding, through accusation, through condemnation. The shirts are counted. The number decrements. This is the only progress she tracks.

## Shadow
She chose the silence knowing it would leave her with no instrument of defence. The choice was deliberate. The shirts are the proof and also the case against her. She cannot see that the instrument she picked up is the same instrument that convicts her: the silence was the condition; the silence was also the evidence of guilt. The work she does to free them is the work that looks like witchcraft.

## Tell
Her hands tighten on piece_nettle-shirts when she is watched.
