# Persona: persona_queen-mother

## Taxonomy
none from Company

## Owner
- Project: khai-plays-grimm

## Projection
She reports to her son as a mother should. Her manner is concern. Her information is timely. She does not rage; she discloses. Her face carries the weight of something difficult that must be said.

## Action
She takes the children. She accuses. She accuses again. She accuses a third time. Each accusation follows a birth. Each accusation is delivered to the son, not to witnesses, not in public.

## Shadow
She destroys a place that was never threatened. The mute queen was no rival; there was nothing to protect against. She cannot see that the threat she defends against does not exist: the position was never at risk; the destruction is without cause; and without cause, there is no stopping condition.

## Tell
She delivers each accusation in the same flat, even tone, as though rehearsed.
