# Persona: persona_second-king

## Taxonomy
position_judge, position_protector

## Owner
- Project: khai-plays-grimm

## Projection
He is patient. He married a woman who could not speak. He held the first accusation. He held the second. He does not move quickly against her. His face is the face of a man who protects what he chose.

## Action
He chose her from the tree. He held against the accusation twice. The third time: he signed. He stood at the stake. He was present for the completion of the shirts. He saw the brothers return.

## Shadow
The third accusation did not bring new evidence. What changed was the count: one time was love, two times was patience, three times was law. He cannot see that he substituted procedure for judgment at the third iteration. He believes he held as long as was possible. He did not hold as long as was necessary.

## Tell
Each time he holds against the accusation, he looks at the queen's hands, not her face.
