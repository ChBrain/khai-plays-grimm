# The Eternal Hunter: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der ewige Jäger_, legend no. 309 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Graf Eberhard encounters a lord cursed to hunt the same stag for centuries with a face dried up like a sponge.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 309.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
