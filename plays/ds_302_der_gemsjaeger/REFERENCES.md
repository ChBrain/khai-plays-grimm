# The Chamois Hunter: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Gemsjäger_, legend no. 302 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A mountain dwarf promises a hunter weekly chamois meat, but kills him when the hunter breaks the pact to hunt again.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 302.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
