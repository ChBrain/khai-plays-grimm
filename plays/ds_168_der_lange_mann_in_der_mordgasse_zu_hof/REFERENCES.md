# The Tall Man in Murder Lane: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der lange Mann in der Mordgasse zu Hof_, legend no. 168 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A giant black man straddling a lane in Hof predicts the coming of the plague before vanishing with a great crash.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 168.
