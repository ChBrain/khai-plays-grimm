# The Phoenix: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Vogel Phönix_ (KHM 75a), an early tale withdrawn from the Grimms' _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_ after the first edition (1812). A youth found by the river escapes a steward's plots and fetches three feathers from the phoenix.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_, first edition (1812), tale KHM 75a. An early tale withdrawn from the later editions. It opens: "Eines Tags ging ein reicher Mann spazieren an den Fluß, da kam ein kleines Kästchen geschwommen..."
