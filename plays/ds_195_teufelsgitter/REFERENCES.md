# The Devil's Grille: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Teufelsgitter_, legend no. 195 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A frustrated blacksmith wishes the devil to finish an intricate baptismal grid, and the devil immediately does so.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 195.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
