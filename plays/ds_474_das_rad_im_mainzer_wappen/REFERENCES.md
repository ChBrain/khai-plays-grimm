# The Wheel in the Mainz Coat of Arms: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Das Rad im Mainzer Wappen_, legend no. 474 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Bishop Willigis, of humble origin, turns his detractors' mocking chalk wheels into his official coat of arms.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 474.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
