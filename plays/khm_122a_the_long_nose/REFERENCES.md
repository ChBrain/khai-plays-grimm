# The Long Nose: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die lange Nase_ (KHM 122a), an early tale withdrawn from the Grimms' _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_ after the first edition (1812). Three soldiers lose their magic gifts to a princess and win them back with a nose-stretching charm.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_, first edition (1812), tale KHM 122a. An early tale withdrawn from the later editions. It opens: "Es waren drei alte abgedankte Soldaten, die waren so alt, daß sie auch keine Libermilch mehr beißen konnten..."
