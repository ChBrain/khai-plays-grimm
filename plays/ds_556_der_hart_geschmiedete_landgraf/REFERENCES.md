# The Hard-Forged Landgrave: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der hart geschmiedete Landgraf_, legend no. 556 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Ludwig gets lost and stays at a Ruhla smithy, where the smith's shouts goad him to become hard like iron.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 556.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 556 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Beyond the legend**: the production stages only what the legend records, not the wider history or tradition it touches.
