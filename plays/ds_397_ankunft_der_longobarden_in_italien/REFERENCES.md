# Arrival of the Lombards in Italy: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Ankunft der Longobarden in Italien_, legend no. 397 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Mocked by the Empress with a golden spindle, Narses retaliates by inviting the Lombards to invade Italy.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 397.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
