# The Wartburg Song Contest: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Wartburger Krieg_, legend no. 561 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Six Minnesingers hold a singing contest at the Wartburg with lethal stakes; Ofterdingen escapes death.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 561.
