# The Murder Castle: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Das Mordschloß_ (KHM 73a), an early tale withdrawn from the Grimms' _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_ after the first edition (1812). A cobbler's daughter follows a rich lord to his castle, uncovers his murders, and escapes in a hay cart.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_, first edition (1812), tale KHM 73a. An early tale withdrawn from the later editions. It opens: "Es war einmal ein Schuhmacher, welcher drei Töchter hatte..."
