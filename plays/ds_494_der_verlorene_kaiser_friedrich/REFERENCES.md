# The Lost Emperor Frederick: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der verlorene Kaiser Friedrich_, legend no. 494 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Excommunicated Emperor Frederick II rides into the forest to hunt and vanishes forever using a magic ring.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 494.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Beyond the legend**: the production stages only what the legend records, not the wider history or tradition it touches.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
