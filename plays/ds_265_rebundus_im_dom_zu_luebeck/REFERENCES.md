# Rebundus in the Cathedral of Lubeck: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Rebundus im Dom zu Lübeck_, legend no. 265 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A Lubeck canon tries to pass a death warning rose to his neighbor, resulting in a curse that shakes his tomb.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 265.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
