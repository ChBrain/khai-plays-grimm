# The Grave of the Saints: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Das Grab der Heiligen_, legend no. 436 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Dagobert finds sanctuary in Saint Denis' chapel after shearing a Duke's beard, escaping Clotaire's wrath.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 436.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Beyond the legend**: the production stages only what the legend records, not the wider history or tradition it touches.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
