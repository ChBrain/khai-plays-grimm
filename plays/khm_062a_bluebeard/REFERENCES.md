# Bluebeard: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Blaubart_ (KHM 62a), an early tale withdrawn from the Grimms' _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_ after the first edition (1812). A young wife breaks her husband Bluebeard's ban and is saved by her brothers at the last moment.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_, first edition (1812), tale KHM 62a. An early tale withdrawn from the later editions. It opens: "In einem Walde lebte ein Mann, der hatte schöne Häuser, silberne und goldene Schlüssel..."
