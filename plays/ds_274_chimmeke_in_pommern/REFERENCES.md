# Chimmeke in Pomerania: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Chimmeke in Pommern_, legend no. 274 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. The Pomeranian house spirit Chimmeke chops up a kitchen boy in Schloss Lonz for eating his daily milk offering.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 274.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
