# The Donkey's Meadow: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die Eselswiese_, legend no. 578 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Saint Bruno's mule stubbornly refuses to move, warning him against his fatal missionary trip to Prussia.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 578.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
