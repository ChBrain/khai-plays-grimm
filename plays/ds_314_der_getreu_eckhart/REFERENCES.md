# The Faithful Eckhart: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der getreu Eckhart_, legend no. 314 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. The Faithful Eckhart leads the spectral Wild Hunt on Fat Tuesday, warning onlookers to clear the way for safety.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 314.
