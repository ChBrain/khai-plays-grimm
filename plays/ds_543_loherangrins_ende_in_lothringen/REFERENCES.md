# Loherangrin's End in Lorraine: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Loherangrins Ende in Lothringen_, legend no. 543 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Loherangrin is stabbed by his wife's suspicious relatives who seek to bind him by a magic ritual.
