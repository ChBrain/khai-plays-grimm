# The Rechenberger's Servant: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Des Rechenbergers Knecht_, legend no. 175 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Hans von Rechenberg has a loyal servant who performs supernatural feats and is revealed to be a helpful spirit.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 175.
