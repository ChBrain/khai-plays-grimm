# The Black Riders and the Led Horse: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die schwarzen Reuter und das Handpferd_, legend no. 313 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A robber knight's servant learns from a black rider of the Wild Hunt that he will die and ride their empty horse.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 313.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 313 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
