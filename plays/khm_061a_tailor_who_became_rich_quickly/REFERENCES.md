# The Tailor Who Became Rich Quickly: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Von dem Schneider, der bald reich wurde_ (KHM 61a), an early tale withdrawn from the Grimms' _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_ after the first edition (1812). A cunning tailor grows hugely rich through fraud and brings the whole village under his thumb.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_, first edition (1812), tale KHM 61a. An early tale withdrawn from the later editions. It opens: "Ein armer Schneider ging einmal zur Winterszeit über das Feld..."
