# Kaiser Karl in the Untersberg: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Kaiser Karl im Untersberg_, legend no. 28 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Kaiser Karl sits in the Untersberg with a golden crown, his beard growing around the table until the end of the world.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 28.

| Source             | Key Work / Event        | Scope                                 |

| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 28 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
