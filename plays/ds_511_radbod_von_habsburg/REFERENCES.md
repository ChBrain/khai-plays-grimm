# Radbod of Habsburg: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Radbod von Habsburg_, legend no. 511 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Graf Radbod builds Habsburg and shows his brother Werner a living wall of loyal vassals.
