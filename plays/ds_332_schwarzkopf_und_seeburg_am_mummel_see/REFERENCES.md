# Schwarzkopf and Seeburg at Lake Mummel: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Schwarzkopf und Seeburg am Mummel-See_, legend no. 332 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Twelve sisters avenge their brother by killing twelve robber barons, leading to their tragic end in Mummelsee.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 332.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
