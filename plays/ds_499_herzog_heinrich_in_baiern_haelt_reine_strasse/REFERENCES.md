# Duke Henry of Bavaria Keeps a Clean Road: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Herzog Heinrich in Baiern hält reine Straße_, legend no. 499 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Duke Henry keeps the roads safe from robbers and smashes clay pots as a warning to corrupt guardians.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 499.
