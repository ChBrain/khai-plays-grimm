# Dagobert and Saint Florentius: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Dagobert und Sanct Florentius_, legend no. 438 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Hermit Florentius bounds wild beasts with wooden rods and cures King Dagobert's blind and mute daughter.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 438.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
