# References: Wie Kinder Schlachtens miteinander gespielt haben

Staged from the early/withdrawn Grimm fairy tale _Wie Kinder Schlachtens miteinander gespielt haben_ (KHM 22a, first edition 1812).

## Source

- **German Title**: Wie Kinder Schlachtens miteinander gespielt haben
- **KHM Number**: 22a
- **Edition**: First Edition (1812), Volume 1, Number 22
- **Primary Source**: [de.wikisource: Wie Kinder Schlachtens miteinander gespielt haben](<https://de.wikisource.org/wiki/Wie_Kinder_Schlachtens_miteinander_gespielt_haben_(1812)>)
- **German Anchor Line**: _"Es war einmal ein Vater, der schlachtete eine Sau, das sahen seine Kinder..."_

## Restrictions

> [!CAUTION]
> **Sensitive Content Framing Warning**
>
> This play represents KHM 22a, a tale containing extreme domestic tragedy and child death.
>
> Under the repository's strict safety guidelines, this playhouse enforces **abstract/structural modeling only**.
>
> - No graphic elements, physical violence, or bodily harm may be represented or modeled in any Plot, Persona, Place, or Piece.
> - The narrative is structured as a game of role-imitation followed by a judicial trial of intent (drawing from the second variant of the 1812 text).
> - Staging of physical suffering or deaths is strictly prohibited.
