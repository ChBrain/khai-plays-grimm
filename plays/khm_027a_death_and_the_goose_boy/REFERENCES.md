# Death and the Goose Boy: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Tod und der Gänshirt_ (KHM 27a), an early tale withdrawn from the Grimms' _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_ after the first edition (1812). A contented goose-boy follows Death to the next world while a miser is punished for his greed.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_, first edition (1812), tale KHM 27a. An early tale withdrawn from the later editions. It opens: "Ein Gänshirt hatte seine Gänse über ein Wasser getrieben..."
