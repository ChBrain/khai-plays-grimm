# Alboin Wins Ticinum: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Alboin gewinnt Ticinum_, legend no. 399 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Alboin's horse collapses at the gate, rising only after the king breaks a vow to massacre the citizens.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 399.
