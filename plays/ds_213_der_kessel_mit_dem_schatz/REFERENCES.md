# The Kettle with the Treasure: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Kessel mit dem Schatz_, legend no. 213 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A rising kettle of gold sinks back under the stove when a girl cries out in surprise instead of keeping silent.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 213.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 213 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |
