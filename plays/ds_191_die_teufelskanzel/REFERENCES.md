# The Devil's Pulpit: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die Teufelskanzel_, legend no. 191 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. The devil once preached to the local people from a row of rocks in Baden called the Devil's Pulpit.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 191.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
