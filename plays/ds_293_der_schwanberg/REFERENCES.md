# The Schwanberg: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Schwanberg_, legend no. 293 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. An ancient German prophecy foretells that Germany will eventually be absorbed entirely into Switzerland.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 293.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
