# The Shoemaker of Lauingen: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Schuster zu Lauingen_, legend no. 473 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A humble shoemaker is chosen by a mysterious voice to fight the pagan champion, winning Pappenheim's arms.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 473.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |
| ------------------ | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 473 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
