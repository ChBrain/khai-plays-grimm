# The Devil's Tower at the Danube Whirlpool: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Teufels-Thurn am Donaustrudel_, legend no. 488 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Bishop Bruno is cursed by a demon at the Danube Strudel; later, the floor collapses and kills him.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 488.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
