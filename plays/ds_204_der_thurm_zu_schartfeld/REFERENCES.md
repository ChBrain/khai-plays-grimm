# The Tower of Schartfeld: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Thurm zu Schartfeld_, legend no. 204 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. The devil throws the roof off the Schartfeld tower to expose a monk who abetted the emperor's adultery.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 204.
