# Theodelind and the Sea Monster: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Theodelind und das Meerwunder_, legend no. 405 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Theodelind is assaulted by a sea monster, giving birth to a dark prince who grows up cruel and malicious.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 405.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Beyond the legend**: the production stages only what the legend records, not the wider history or tradition it touches.
