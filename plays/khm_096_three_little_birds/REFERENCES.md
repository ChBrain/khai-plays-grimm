# The Three Little Birds: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _De drei Vügelkens_ (KHM 096) as an illustration of how a calumniated truth resurfaces and overturns a wrongful judgment. The production models the contrast between the false report whispered by the envious sisters and the prophetic bird's revealing song, tracing how the children rescued from the water carry the hidden truth back to the king and free their wasted mother.

## Origin

Jacob and Wilhelm Grimm's _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_ (1812-1857), tale KHM 096, in the definitive 7th edition of 1857. The Low German title is "De drei Vügelkens".

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Hydrology of the waters**: the river of the drownings and the great water are treated as topological passages of loss and crossing, ignoring currents, depth, or fish.
- **Mechanics of the enchantment**: the black dog's transformation is modeled as a state change triggered by silence and the wand, rather than any rule system of magic.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
