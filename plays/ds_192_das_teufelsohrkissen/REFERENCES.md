# The Devil's Ear Pillow: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Das Teufelsohrkissen_, legend no. 192 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A flat stone at the foot of Castle Bentheim contains the ear print of the devil who once slept upon it.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 192.
