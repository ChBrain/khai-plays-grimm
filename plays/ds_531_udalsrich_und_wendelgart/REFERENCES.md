# Udalrich and Wendelgart: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Udalrich und Wendelgart und der ungeborne Burkard_, legend no. 531 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Udalrich returns from captivity; a crabapple miracle by Wiborad foretells his return to Wendelgart.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 531.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 531 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
