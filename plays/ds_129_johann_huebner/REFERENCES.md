# Johann Huebner: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Johann Hübner_, legend no. 129 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. The one-eyed robber Johann Huebner terrorizes a Westphalian forest from his castle until he is killed in battle.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 129.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
