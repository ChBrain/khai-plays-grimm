# Thalmann von Lunderstedt: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Thalmann von Lunderstedt_, legend no. 579 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Cornered on a cliff by his enemies, Thalmann spurs his horse off the precipice into the Saale river.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 579.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
