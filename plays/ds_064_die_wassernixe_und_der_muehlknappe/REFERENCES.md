# The Water Nix and the Miller's Apprentice: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die Wassernixe und der Mühlknappe_, legend no. 64 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. An apprentice shoots at a nix combing her hair on the river; three days later, he drowns while bathing.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 64.

| Source             | Key Work / Event        | Scope                                 |
| ------------------ | ----------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 64 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
