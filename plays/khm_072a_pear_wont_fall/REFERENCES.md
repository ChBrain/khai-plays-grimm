# The Pear Won't Fall: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Das Birnli will nit fallen_ (KHM 72a), an early tale withdrawn from the Grimms' _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_ after the first edition (1812). A master tries in vain to shake a pear down through disobedient helpers, until the knacker is called.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_, first edition (1812), tale KHM 72a. An early tale withdrawn from the later editions. It opens: "Der Herr will das Birnli schüttle, das Birnli will nit fallen: der Herr, der schickt das Jockli hinaus, es soll das Birnli schüttle..."
