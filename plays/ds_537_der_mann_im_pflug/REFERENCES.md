# The Man in the Plow: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Mann im Pflug_, legend no. 537 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A knight is forced to plow fields in captivity, but his wife's woven shirt remains miraculously clean.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 537.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 537 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
