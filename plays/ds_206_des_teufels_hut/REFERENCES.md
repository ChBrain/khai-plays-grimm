# The Devil's Hat: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Des Teufels Hut_, legend no. 206 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. The devil boasts of wearing a massive stone as a hat until Christ easily lifts it with a single finger.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 206.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |
| ------------------ | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 206 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Beyond the legend**: the production stages only what the legend records, not the wider history or tradition it touches.
