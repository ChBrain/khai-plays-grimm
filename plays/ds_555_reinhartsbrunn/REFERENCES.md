# Reinhartsbrunn: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Reinhartsbrunn_, legend no. 555 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Ludwig founds Reinhartsbrunn Abbey after a potter informs him of two mysterious lights seen at night.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 555.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
