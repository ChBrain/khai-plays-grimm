# Dagobert's Soul in the Boat: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Dagoberts Seele im Schiff_, legend no. 439 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Saints intercept devils on the sea, rescuing King Dagobert's soul from being carried to hell.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 439.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
