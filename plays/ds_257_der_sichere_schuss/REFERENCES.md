# The Sure Shot: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der sichere Schuß_, legend no. 257 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A magic marksman is able to hit any named target within range, even if he fires at random through a window.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 257.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
