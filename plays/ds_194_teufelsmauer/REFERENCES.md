# Devil's Wall: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Teufelsmauer_, legend no. 194 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. The devil tries to build a stone wall across the Danube, but the stones constantly slip out of his hands.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 194.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
