# Gelimer in Silver Chains: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Gelimer in silberner Kette_, legend no. 377 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Vandal King Gelimer is outwitted with a silver chain by Belisarius and proves his courage in a combat trial.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 377.
