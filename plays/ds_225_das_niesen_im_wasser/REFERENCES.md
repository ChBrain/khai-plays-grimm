# The Sneezing in the Water: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Das Niesen im Wasser_, legend no. 225 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A spirit sneezing in the Auerbach is freed when a traveler blesses him but a curse falls on a cursing traveler.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 225.
