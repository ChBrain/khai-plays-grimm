# The Devil Carries the Bride Away: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Teufel führt die Braut fort_, legend no. 209 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A rich woman breaks her wedding vow to her poor lover and is carried off by the devil during her wedding dance.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 209.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |
| ------------------ | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 209 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
