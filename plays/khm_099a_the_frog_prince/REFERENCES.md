# The Frog Prince: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Froschprinz_ (KHM 99a), an early tale withdrawn from the Grimms' _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_ after the first edition (1812). The youngest princess promises a frog her love for clear water and frees him after three nights.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_, first edition (1812), tale KHM 99a. An early tale withdrawn from the later editions. It opens: "Es war einmal ein König, der hatte drei Töchter, in seinem Hof aber stand ein Brunnen mit schönem klarem Wasser."
