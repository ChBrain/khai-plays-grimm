# Sage of Irmenfried, Iring and Dieterich: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Sage von Irmenfried, Iring und Dieterich_, legend no. 551 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. The Thuringian advisor Iring kills his King Irmenfried for Dieterich, but avenges him when betrayed.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 551.
