# The First Accusations: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _The First Accusations_, an episode from Jacob Grimm's _Reinhart Fuchs_. The animals bring their formal complaints to the court of King Nobel.

## Origin

Jacob Grimm's edition of the animal epic _Reinhart Fuchs_ (1834).

| Source          | Key Work / Event        | Scope                                           |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
