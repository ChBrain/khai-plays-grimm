# The Betrothal of Ludwig and Elisabeth: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die Vermählung der Kinder Ludwig und Elisabeth_, legend no. 563 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Hungarian Princess Elisabeth is betrothed to young Ludwig and sent to Thuringia in a silver cradle.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 563.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |
| ------------------ | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 563 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
