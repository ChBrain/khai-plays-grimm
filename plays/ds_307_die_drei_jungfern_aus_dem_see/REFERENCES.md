# The Three Maidens from the Lake: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die drei Jungfern aus dem See_, legend no. 307 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A youth sets the village clock back to keep three lake maidens longer, leading to their tragic deaths in the water.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 307.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |
| ------------------ | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 307 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
