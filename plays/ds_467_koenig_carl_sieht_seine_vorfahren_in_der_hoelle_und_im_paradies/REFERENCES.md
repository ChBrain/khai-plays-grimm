# King Charles's Vision of Hell and Paradise: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _König Carl sieht seine Vorfahren in der Hölle und im Paradies_, legend no. 467 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. King Charles the Fat is guided by an angel through hellish sulfur wells and paradise valleys.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 467.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 467 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Beyond the legend**: the production stages only what the legend records, not the wider history or tradition it touches.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
