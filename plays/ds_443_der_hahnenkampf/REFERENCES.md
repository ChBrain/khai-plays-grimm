# The Cockfight: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Hahnenkampf_, legend no. 443 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Queen Hildegard settles the royal succession dispute between her sons using a cockfight.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 443.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
