# Henry with the Golden Plow: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Heinrich mit dem goldenen Pfluge_, legend no. 525 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Henry claims a large forest territory by encircling it with a golden plow during the Emperor's nap.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 525.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
