# Hurleburlebutz: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Hurleburlebutz_ (KHM 66a), an early tale withdrawn from the Grimms' _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_ after the first edition (1812). A lost king promises his youngest daughter to a wood-imp who exposes the true bride by a test.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_, first edition (1812), tale KHM 66a. An early tale withdrawn from the later editions. It opens: "Ein König verirrte sich auf der Jagd, da trat ein kleines weißes Männchen vor ihn..."
