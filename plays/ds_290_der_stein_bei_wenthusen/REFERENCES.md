# The Stone near Wenthusen: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Stein bei Wenthusen_, legend no. 290 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A cursed monastery stone brings severe misfortune to any owner who dares to move it from its designated place.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 290.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |
| ------------------ | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 290 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Beyond the legend**: the production stages only what the legend records, not the wider history or tradition it touches.
