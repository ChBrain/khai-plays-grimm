# Hatred Between Saxons and Swabians: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Haß zwischen den Sachsen und Schwaben_, legend no. 420 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Swabians take Goth wives during their campaign, causing a return war and disinheriting the traitorous women.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 420.
