# Frederick has his Daughter Nursed: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Markgraf Friedrich läßt seine Tochter säugen_, legend no. 567 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Under siege, Friedrich breakout and halts his retreat repeatedly to let a wet-nurse feed his baby.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 567.
