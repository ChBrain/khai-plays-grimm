# Dove Keeps the Enemy Away: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Taube hält den Feind ab_, legend no. 125 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A dove prevents a banneret from firing a cannon during a siege, saving the city of Hoxter until Swedish forces arrive.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 125.
