# Duke Henry and the Golden Necklace: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Herzog Heinrich und die goldne Halskette_, legend no. 469 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Duke Henry avoids a lethal strangulation trap after a smith warns him of Hatto's golden collar.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 469.
