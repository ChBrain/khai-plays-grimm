# God Weeps with the Innocent: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Gott weint mit dem Unschuldigen_, legend no. 361 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A rain shower falls from a clear blue sky to witness the innocence of an accused woman at her execution site.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 361.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
