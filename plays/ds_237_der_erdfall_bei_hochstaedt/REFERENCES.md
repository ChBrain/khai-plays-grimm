# The Sinkhole near Hochstaedt: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Erdfall bei Hochstädt_, legend no. 237 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Angry boys who stomp and whip their bread are swallowed up by a sudden sinkhole while the innocent boy escapes.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 237.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 237 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
