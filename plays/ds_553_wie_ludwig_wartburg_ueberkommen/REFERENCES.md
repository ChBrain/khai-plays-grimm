# How Ludwig Obtained the Wartburg: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Wie Ludwig Wartburg überkommen_, legend no. 553 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Ludwig the Springer claims a steep hill by carrying Thuringian soil to it and swearing a literal oath.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 553.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
