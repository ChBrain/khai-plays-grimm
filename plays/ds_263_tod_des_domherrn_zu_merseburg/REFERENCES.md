# Death of the Canon of Merseburg: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Tod des Domherrn zu Merseburg_, legend no. 263 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A mysterious thunderous blow strikes a canon's seat in Merseburg Cathedral three weeks before his death.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 263.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |
| ------------------ | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 263 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Beyond the legend**: the production stages only what the legend records, not the wider history or tradition it touches.
