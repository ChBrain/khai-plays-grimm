# The Old Wine Cellar near Salurn: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der alte Weinkeller bei Salurn_, legend no. 15 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Christoph finds a wine cellar guarded by three old men, but after revealing the secret, he is killed.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 15.
