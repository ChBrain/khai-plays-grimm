# Poor Heinrich: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Poor Heinrich_, Hartmann von Aue's Middle High German poem in the Brothers Grimm's edition. Heinrich the knight is struck with leprosy and is miraculously healed when he refuses the sacrifice of a pure maiden.

## Origin

Jacob and Wilhelm Grimm's edition of Hartmann von Aue's _Der arme Heinrich_ (1815).

| Source               | Key Work / Event                      | Scope                                      |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| **Hartmann von Aue** | _Der arme Heinrich_ (Grimm ed., 1815) | The medieval poem as the Grimms edited it. |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **The verse form**: the production models the poem's action, not its Middle High German metre or diction.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
