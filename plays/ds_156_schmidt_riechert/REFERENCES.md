# Smith Riechert: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Schmidt Riechert_, legend no. 156 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A smith accidentally cracks a dwarf's skull with a threshing flail after guarding his pea field against theft.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 156.
