# The Queen in the Wax Smock: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die Königin im Wachshemd_, legend no. 465 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Queen Richardis proves her innocence against false adultery charges by surviving a trial by fire in a wax smock.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 465.
