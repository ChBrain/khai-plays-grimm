# Remigius Expels the Fire: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Remig verjagt die Feuersbrunst_, legend no. 428 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Remigius' footprints press into stone as he drives a raging fire out of Rheims and locks the city gate.
