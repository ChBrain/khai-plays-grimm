# The Garlic King: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Knoblauchskönig_, legend no. 491 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Saxons reject Henry's son and elect counter-king Hermann, mockingly dubbed 'Garlic King' at Eisleben.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 491.
