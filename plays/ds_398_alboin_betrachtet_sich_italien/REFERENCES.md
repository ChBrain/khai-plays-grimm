# Alboin Surveys Italy: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Alboin betrachtet sich Italien_, legend no. 398 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Alboin climbs a mountain named Königsberg to survey Italy, a land rumored to house giant wisents.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 398.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
