# Why the Goths Invaded Greece: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Warum die Gothen in Griechenland eingebrochen_, legend no. 371 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. The removal of three buried silver statues guarding the northern border triggers a barbarian invasion of Thrace.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 371.
