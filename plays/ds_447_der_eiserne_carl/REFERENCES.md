# The Iron Charlemagne: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der eiserne Carl_, legend no. 447 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. King Desiderius watches in terror from a tower as the iron-clad army of Charlemagne approaches.
