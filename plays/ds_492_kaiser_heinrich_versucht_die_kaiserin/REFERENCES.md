# Emperor Henry Tests the Empress: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Kaiser Heinrich versucht die Kaiserin_, legend no. 492 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Emperor Henry V tests his wife's fidelity in disguise; she discovers the ruse and orders him beaten.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 492.
