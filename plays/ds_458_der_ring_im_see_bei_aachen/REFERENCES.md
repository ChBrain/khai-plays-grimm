# The Ring in the Lake at Aachen: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Ring im See bei Aachen_, legend no. 458 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Charlemagne's magic ring shifted to a lake, drawing him to build his palace on its shore.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 458.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
