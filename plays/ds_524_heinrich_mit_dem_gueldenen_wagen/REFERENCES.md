# Henry with the Golden Cart: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Heinrich mit dem güldenen Wagen_, legend no. 524 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Heinrich uses a golden cart and fresh relays of horses to encircle and claim a vast Bavarian estate.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 524.
