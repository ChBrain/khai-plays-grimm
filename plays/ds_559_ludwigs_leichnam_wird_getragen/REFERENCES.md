# Ludwig's Corpse is Carried: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Ludwigs Leichnam wird getragen_, legend no. 559 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Fearing him even in death, the rebellious nobles carry Ludwig's coffin ten miles on their shoulders.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 559.
