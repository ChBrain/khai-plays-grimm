# Duchess Sophie's Glove: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Frau Sophiens Handschuh_, legend no. 565 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Sophie throws her glove, declaring Hessen's loyalty, after the Markgraf of Meißen refuses her claims.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 565.
