# The Horses from the Loft Hole: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die Pferde aus dem Bodenloch_, legend no. 341 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A mayor's resurrected wife is let inside only after his white horses miraculously climb to the hayloft.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 341.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |
| ------------------ | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| **Brothers Grimm** | _Deutsche Sagen_, DS 341 | The legend as the Grimms set it down. |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
