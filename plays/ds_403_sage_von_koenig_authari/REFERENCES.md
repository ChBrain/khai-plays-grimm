# Saga of King Authari: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Sage von König Authari_, legend no. 403 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. King Authari wooes Bavarian princess Theodelind in disguise, revealing his identity with a touch.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 403.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Beyond the legend**: the production stages only what the legend records, not the wider history or tradition it touches.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
