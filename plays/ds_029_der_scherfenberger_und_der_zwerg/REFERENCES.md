# Scherfenberger and the Dwarf: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Scherfenberger und der Zwerg_, legend no. 29 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Knight Wilhelm von Scherfenberg receives a protective ring from a dwarf king, but dies on the battlefield.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 29.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
