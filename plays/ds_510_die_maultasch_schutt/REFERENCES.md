# The Maultasch Ruins: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die Maultasch-Schutt_, legend no. 510 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Defenders trick Margaretha Maultasch by throwing a fed ox over the wall to fake abundance.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 510.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
