# Emperor Otto Holds Widow's Court: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Kaiser Otto hält Witwen- und Waisengericht_, legend no. 480 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A executed count's widow proves his innocence by carrying a red-hot iron, demanding the Empress's execution.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 480.
