# The Cup with the Notch: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Kelch mit der Scharte_, legend no. 485 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Saint Lawrence throws a golden chalice onto the scales of judgment to save Emperor Henry's soul, leaving a notch.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 485.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
