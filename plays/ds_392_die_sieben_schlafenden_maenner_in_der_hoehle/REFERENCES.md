# The Seven Sleepers in the Cave: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die sieben schlafenden Männer in der Höhle_, legend no. 392 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Seven Roman sleepers in a coastal cave remain incorruptible, protected by a curse against thieves.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 392.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
