# Die Göttinger Sieben: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Die Göttinger Sieben_, the historical episode of the Göttingen Seven (1837). The historic defiance, protestation, and banishment of the Seven Professors in 1837.

## Origin

The historical protest of the Göttingen Seven (1837), the seven professors -- Jacob and Wilhelm Grimm among them -- dismissed for their stand against the King of Hanover.
