# Of King Charles and the Frisians: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Von König Carl und den Friesen_, legend no. 450 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Charlemagne wins Frisia from King Radbod in a standing contest by picking up a dropped glove.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 450.

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Beyond the legend**: the production stages only what the legend records, not the wider history or tradition it touches.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
