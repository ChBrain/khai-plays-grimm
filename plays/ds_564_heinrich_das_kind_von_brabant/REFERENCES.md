# Henry the Child of Brabant: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Heinrich das Kind von Brabant_, legend no. 564 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A war of succession breaks out as the Hessians support Saint Elisabeth's infant son Heinrich for ruler.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 564.
