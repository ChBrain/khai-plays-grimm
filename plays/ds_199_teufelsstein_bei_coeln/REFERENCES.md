# The Devil's Stone near Cologne: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Teufelsstein bei Cöln_, legend no. 199 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. The devil attempts to destroy a chapel in Cologne by throwing a heavy stone but fails completely.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 199.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
