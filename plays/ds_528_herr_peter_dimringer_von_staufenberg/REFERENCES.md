# Peter Dimringer of Staufenberg: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Herr Peter Dimringer von Staufenberg_, legend no. 528 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Peter makes a pact with a fairy bride but dies on his wedding day to a princess when he breaks his vow.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 528.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
