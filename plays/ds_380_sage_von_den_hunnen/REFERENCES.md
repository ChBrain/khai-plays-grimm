# Saga of the Huns: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Sage von den Hunnen_, legend no. 380 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Rumors attribute the Huns' origin to exile, a ransom horse, or a union between a wizard and a she-wolf.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 380.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |
| ------------------ | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.

- **Beyond the legend**: the production stages only what the legend records, not the wider history or tradition it touches.
- **Cross-play context**: strict local isolation. Every link stays within this play's directory.
