# Duke Bundus, called the Wolf: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Herzog Bundus, genannt der Wolf_, legend no. 523 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Duke Balthasar passes off a hunter's son as his own; the truth is revealed but Bundus retains his duchy.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 523.
