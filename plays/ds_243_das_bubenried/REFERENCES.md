# The Boys' Clearing: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Das Bubenried_, legend no. 243 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Two begging boys who refuse to share a small loaf of bread fight and beat each other to death with fence stakes.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 243.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
