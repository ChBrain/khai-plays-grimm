# The Stone Table at Bingenheim: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Steintisch zu Bingenheim_, legend no. 167 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. A linden tree court in Bingenheim features a stone table left by forest-dwelling giants who left handprints behind.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 167.

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
