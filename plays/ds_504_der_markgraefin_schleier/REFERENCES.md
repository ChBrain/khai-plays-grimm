# The Margravine's Veil: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Der Markgräfin Schleier_, legend no. 504 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Leopold vows to build a monastery where his wife's wind-blown veil is found, discovering it 8 years later.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 504.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

## Restrictions

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
