# Clotilda's Betrothal: Reference

## Line of Work

Staging _Crothilds Verlobung_, legend no. 430 of the Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_. Envoy Aurelian disguises as a beggar to slip Clovis' betrothal ring to Burgundian princess Clotilda.

## Origin

The Brothers Grimm's _Deutsche Sagen_ (1816-1818), legend no. 430.

| Source             | Key Work / Event         | Scope                                 |

What the production refuses to model, and to whom it delegates.
