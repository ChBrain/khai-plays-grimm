# Persona: Wilhelm Grimm

## Taxonomy

[The Playwright](position_playwright.md). Stamped for the Grimm house.

## Owner

- Project: khai-plays-grimm

## Projection

The younger brother who shapes the tales to be told around the hearth. He believes a story must live in the ear of the child, and edits the collection to ensure it is morally acceptable and poetically satisfying.

## Action

He rewrites and expands. He adds descriptive details and moral lessons to the plays. He debates with Jacob to soften the harshness of the original oral reports.

## Shadow

He sanitizes the folklore. By cleaning up the violence and sexuality in the tales, he obscures the darker, older truths of the oral tradition.

## Tell

He speaks of children, the hearth, the beauty of the story, and the moral lesson.
