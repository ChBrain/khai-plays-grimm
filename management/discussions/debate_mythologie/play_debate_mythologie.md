# Play: Deutsche Mythologie Debate

## Estate

[khai-plays-grimm](https://github.com/ChBrain/khai-plays-grimm): the repository that holds this production and answers for it.

## Name

Deutsche Mythologie Debate.

## Arc

Nicias and Pericles debate how to represent a non-narrative mythological tract as plays in the house, deciding to model the 38 chapters of the work as conceptual plays representing the cosmological rule-sets of the Grimm corpus.

## Triggers

**[Plot: Plan](plot_01_plan.md)**

Nicias and Pericles lay out their conflicting plans: Nicias cautions that a non-narrative reference work cannot be staged without violating the prose standard, while Pericles asserts that myth is the rule-set underlying all tales and must be staged.

**[Plot: Do](plot_02_do.md)**

The debate focuses on the structural breakdown: mapping the 38 chapters of _Deutsche Mythologie_ to `dm_NNN_` plays, where each play defines the rules for a specific category of belief (gods, giants, fate, curses).

**[Plot: Check](plot_03_check.md)**

They verify how these rules will interface with KHM and DS, ensuring that the validation engine remains green and the language policy (English for schema, German for prose) is upheld.

**[Plot: Act](plot_04_act.md)**

The debate concludes with the release of the final staging guidelines, directing the Playwright to author `PRODUCTION_mythologie.md`.

## Stakes

Whether the playhouse can model cosmological belief as systemic rules without diluting its narrative focus.
