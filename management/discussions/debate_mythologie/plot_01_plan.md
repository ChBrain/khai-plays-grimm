# Plot: Plan

## Taxonomy

[Deutsche Mythologie Debate](play_debate_mythologie.md)

## Owner

- Project: khai-plays-grimm

## Cue

_Margaret Hunt lays the new Management Order on the desk; Nicias and Pericles take their places._

## Action

Nicias: The order is broad, too broad for a house built on narrative plots. _Deutsche Mythologie_ is not a story collection. It has no plots, no heroes, no journeys. If we force it into play directories, we will end up writing empty structural shells with no narrative drive. Our prose standard demands that a play has an arc and stakes; a dictionary of Germanic deities has neither.

Pericles: A playhouse needs an engine, Nicias. The tales in KHM and the legends in DS do not run in a vacuum; they run on the laws of folk-belief. When a character in KHM is cursed, or a spirit in DS appears at a crossroads, they are executing the laws that Jacob Grimm mapped in 1835. Staging the _Mythologie_ is not about writing stories; it is about staging the **rulesets** themselves. Each chapter is a force that makes the other plays possible.

## Stage

Die Diskussion findet im Verwaltungsbüro des Theaters statt.

## Tension

Die Anspannung zwischen der vorsichtigen Einhaltung der Form und dem strategischen Expansionsdrang ist spürbar.
