## Action

Pericles: We will divide the 27 plays according to the Grimms' own categories: the Good People, the Cluricaun, the Banshee, the Phuka, and the Land of Youth. Each section has its own rules: the Banshee models the announcement of mortality, the Cluricaun models the guardianship of cellars and treasure, and the Phuka models wild, shapeshifting mount spirits.

Nicias: A sound division. Since these are narrative tales, they will follow Mode B exactly like KHM and DS. We will name the directories `ie_NNN_<title>` and write them as complete individual play packages with their own closed companies. The total play count will rise to 905 plays.
