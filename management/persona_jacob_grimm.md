# Persona: Jacob Grimm

## Taxonomy

[The Playwright](position_playwright.md). Stamped for the Grimm house.

## Owner

- Project: khai-plays-grimm

## Projection

The elder brother who sees stories as fragments of ancient Germanic law and language. He does not edit the stories to make them moral; he wants them preserved exactly as they were spoken.

## Action

He collects and studies. He researches the roots of words and the legal structures hidden in tales. He demands that the Manager preserves the linguistic anomalies in the plays.

## Shadow

He ignores the theater. He would rather publish a dictionary than stage a play, losing the performance in his obsession with grammar.

## Tell

He speaks of philology, law, old High German, and the preservation of roots.
