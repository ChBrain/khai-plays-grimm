# Deutsche Sagen: a deferred expansion of the house

A management plan, not a play, and not a completed order (an order's targets
must all be resolved; this mandate is still open). It records the Choregos'
standing intent to extend this same house, in a later season, to the brothers'
_Deutsche Sagen_ (German Legends, 1816 - 1818, roughly 585 entries).

## Mandate

After the _Kinder- und Hausmärchen_ season is sealed at 255 plays (the 210 tales
of the 1857 edition of last hand plus the 45 withdrawn and early-edition tales)
and released, this house is to be extended to the _Deutsche Sagen_ as a further
season within the same repository. The start date is unset; the season is held
until the KHM is sealed and the Choregos give the word. This document changes
nothing in `plays/`; it is the record of intent.

## When it activates

- Only after the KHM season is complete (255 plays) and the release is cut.
- On the Choregos' explicit word, not automatically.

## How it runs

- Same pipeline, unchanged: a grounded primary public-domain text with an anchor
  line, then a khai play through the gates, merged one branch at a time onto
  current `main`.
- The authoritative _Deutsche Sagen_ roster and count are confirmed against a
  citable scholarly edition first (as Uther serves for the KHM), so the target
  is exact before staging.
- The legends sit in this house under a numbering that does not disturb the KHM
  play-count and version scheme - a distinct id-space or section, decided at
  activation.
- Sensitive legends get the same structural framing the house applies to
  sensitive tales: encode, do not reproduce graphically, and note it in
  REFERENCES.
