play: stage KHM 60a (withdrawn) in Grimm-Deutsch - poor broom-binder children find a bird laying a golden egg a day and grow rich selling them - the bird asks to be taken to the goldsmith and sings that its heart makes a king and its liver brings a gold purse each morning - the goldsmith plots to marry the sister and roast the bird, but heart and liver fall out and are eaten by the children, on whom the prophecy comes true.
