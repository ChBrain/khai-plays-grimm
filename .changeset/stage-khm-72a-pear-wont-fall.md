play: stage KHM 72a in Grimm-Deutsch - Das Birnli will nit fallen - the master wants to shake a pear from the tree but it will not fall - he sends Jockli, then Hündli, Prügeli, Fürli, Wässerli, Kälbli, Metzger, and Schinder - each refusing the command until the Schinder's threat of hanging the Metzger reverses the refusal and causes the pear to fall.
